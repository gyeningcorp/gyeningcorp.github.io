Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Rolling In ("the App") is operated by gyeningcorp. This page explains what limited data the App uses and why.

What we collect

Nothing on our servers. The App is a static website. We do not operate a backend, we do not run analytics, and we do not place tracking cookies.

Data that stays on your device

  • Location (optional). If you tap "Detect my location," your browser asks for GPS permission. Your coordinates are sent one time to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim to convert them into a city name. We never see or store your coordinates.
  • Preferences. Your chosen city and theme are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. Clearing your browser data removes them.

Third-party services the App may contact

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — reverse geocoding of coordinates to a city.
  • Public CORS proxies (allorigins.win, corsproxy.io, thingproxy.freeboard.io) — used to fetch public venue websites on your behalf so their event pages can be parsed in your browser.
  • Venue websites and social platforms — linked from the app. Their own privacy policies apply once you click a link.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
